# 4.1 Understanding Stakeholder Priorities

## Core Concept
Learning what's important to different stakeholders and how to align product decisions with their priorities.

## Key Learning Points
- **Stakeholder Mapping**: Identifying all relevant stakeholders and their influence
- **Priority Identification**: Understanding what each stakeholder values most
- **Alignment Strategies**: Aligning product decisions with stakeholder priorities
- **Influence Techniques**: Positively influencing stakeholder decisions

## Stakeholder Types
- **Executives**: Focus on business impact, ROI, and strategic alignment
- **Engineering**: Focus on technical feasibility, performance, and maintainability
- **Marketing**: Focus on user acquisition, positioning, and competitive advantage
- **Sales**: Focus on customer needs, objections, and closing deals
- **Support**: Focus on user satisfaction, issue resolution, and ease of use

## Practical Applications
- **Stakeholder Analysis**: Mapping stakeholders and their priorities
- **Communication Tailoring**: Adapting messages for different audiences
- **Priority Alignment**: Ensuring product decisions support stakeholder goals
- **Influence Planning**: Developing strategies to influence key stakeholders

## Exercises
1. **Stakeholder Mapping**: Map all stakeholders and their priorities
2. **Message Tailoring**: Create different messages for different stakeholders
3. **Alignment Planning**: Develop strategies to align with stakeholder priorities

## Key Takeaways
- Different stakeholders have different priorities and concerns
- Tailored communication is more effective than one-size-fits-all messaging
- Alignment with stakeholder priorities builds support for decisions
- Understanding influence networks helps identify key decision-makers
