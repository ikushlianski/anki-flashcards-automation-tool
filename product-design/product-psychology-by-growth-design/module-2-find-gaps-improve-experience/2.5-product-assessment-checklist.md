# 2.5 Product Assessment Checklist

## Core Tool
A step-by-step system to grade any product experience using psychological principles.

## Assessment Categories
1. **Clarity**: How clear is the value proposition and next steps?
2. **Motivation**: How well does the experience motivate users to act?
3. **Ability**: How easy is it for users to complete desired actions?
4. **Trust**: How much do users trust the product and company?
5. **Emotion**: How do users feel about their experience?
6. **Memory**: How well will users remember this experience?

## Practical Applications
- **Experience Auditing**: Systematically evaluating product experiences
- **Improvement Prioritization**: Identifying the most impactful changes
- **Benchmarking**: Comparing experiences across different products
- **Progress Tracking**: Measuring improvements over time

## Exercises
1. **Full Assessment**: Complete the checklist for your product
2. **Gap Prioritization**: Rank identified gaps by impact and effort
3. **Improvement Planning**: Create a plan to address top gaps

## Key Takeaways
- Systematic assessment reveals hidden opportunities
- All six categories must be considered for complete evaluation
- Prioritization ensures focus on highest-impact improvements
- Regular assessment tracks progress over time
