# 1.1 Storytelling Psychology

## Core Concept
Using the psychology behind stories to build user empathy and find experience gaps.

## Key Learning Points
- **Narrative Psychology**: How stories shape user perception and behavior
- **Empathy Building**: Creating emotional connections through storytelling
- **Gap Identification**: Using stories to uncover hidden user needs
- **User Journey Narratives**: Framing experiences as compelling stories

## Practical Applications
- **6P Story Framework**: Structure for customer story analysis
  - **Person**: Who is the user?
  - **Problem**: What challenge do they face?
  - **Place**: Where does this happen?
  - **Process**: How do they currently solve it?
  - **Product**: How does your solution fit?
  - **Payoff**: What's the desired outcome?

## Exercises
1. **Customer Story Mapping**: Write a 6P story for your target user
2. **Empathy Gap Analysis**: Identify where your story differs from user reality
3. **Narrative Testing**: Test different story angles with users

## Key Takeaways
- Stories create emotional connections that data alone cannot
- The 6P framework provides a structured approach to understanding users
- Narrative gaps often reveal the most important product opportunities
- Testing different story angles helps uncover diverse user perspectives
