# Module 1: Requirements Fundamentals

## Overview
This module establishes the foundation for understanding product requirements. You'll learn the core concepts, types, and hierarchy of requirements that form the backbone of successful product development.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand what product requirements are and why they matter
- Distinguish between different types of requirements
- Learn the requirements hierarchy and how to structure them
- Master stakeholder analysis for effective requirements gathering
- Differentiate between requirements, specifications, and user stories

## Topics Covered
1. [What are Product Requirements?](./1.1-what-are-product-requirements.md)
2. [Types of Requirements (Functional vs Non-Functional)](./1.2-types-of-requirements.md)
3. [The Requirements Hierarchy](./1.3-requirements-hierarchy.md)
4. [Stakeholder Analysis for Requirements](./1.4-stakeholder-analysis.md)
5. [Requirements vs Specifications vs User Stories](./1.5-requirements-vs-specifications.md)

## Key Takeaways
- Requirements are the foundation of successful products
- Different types of requirements serve different purposes
- Proper stakeholder analysis ensures comprehensive requirements
- Clear distinction between requirements, specs, and user stories is crucial
