# Product Psychology Masterclass
## The step-by-step system to apply product psychology

**Goal**: Learn to apply the right psychological principle to improve any product experience.

---

## Module 1: Understand User Behaviors (Week 1)

### Core Concepts
- **Storytelling Psychology**: Using the psychology behind stories to build user empathy and find gaps
- **Behavioral Map**: Motivating people to act ethically while understanding what impacts their ability (5 elements that can reduce psychology)
- **Psych Framework**: Advanced applications of motivations and abilities to represent user behaviors

### Key Mental Models
1. **6P Story Framework**: The best way to frame customer stories to find new gaps in experience
2. **GEQs (3 Questions)**: Exact questions to uncover deep customer needs
3. **Behavior Map**: Finding if any experience is above the activation threshold

### Practical Applications
- Practice the Psych Framework on real screens
- Compare exercises with proposed solutions
- Build user empathy through storytelling

---

## Module 2: Find The Gaps & Improve Any Experience (Weeks 2-3)

### Core Framework: B.I.A.S.
**The 4 mental steps people go through when interacting with any product:**

1. **B** - Beliefs (what they think)
2. **I** - Intentions (what they plan to do)
3. **A** - Actions (what they actually do)
4. **S** - Satisfaction (how they feel about it)

### Key Learning Areas
- Understanding fast processes vs. logical thinking
- How cognitive biases affect product usage
- Identifying what doesn't work and why
- Using impactful psychology principles to improve products
- Helping people understand, take action, and store positive memories

### Practical Tools
- **Product Assessment Checklist**: Step-by-step grading system for any experience
- **Case Study Assets**: Building product case studies using proprietary story format
- **B.I.A.S. Framework Case Study**: Hands-on practice with redesigns

### Learning Outcomes

## Learning Methodology

### For Each Module:
1. **Theory**: Deep dive into psychological principles
2. **Practice**: Hands-on exercises and templates
3. **Application**: Real-world case studies
4. **Assessment**: Compare your work with expert solutions
5. **Reflection**: Understand what works and why

### Success Metrics:
- Ability to identify psychological gaps in any product
- Confidence in applying appropriate psychological principles
- Skills in creating ethical, user-centered experiences
- Capability to communicate decisions effectively
