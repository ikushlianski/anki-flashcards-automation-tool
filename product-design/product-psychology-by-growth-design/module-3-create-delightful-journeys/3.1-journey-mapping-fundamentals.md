# 3.1 Journey Mapping Fundamentals

## Core Concept
Understanding the complete customer journey and identifying high-impact moments.

## Key Learning Points
- **Journey Stages**: Awareness, Consideration, Purchase, Onboarding, Usage, Retention
- **Touchpoint Analysis**: Every interaction between user and product
- **Emotional Mapping**: How users feel at each stage
- **Pain Point Identification**: Where users struggle or feel frustrated
- **Opportunity Recognition**: Where to create delight

## Practical Applications
- **Current State Mapping**: Documenting existing user journeys
- **Gap Analysis**: Finding where journeys break down
- **Opportunity Prioritization**: Identifying highest-impact improvements
- **Stakeholder Alignment**: Getting team buy-in on journey priorities

## Exercises
1. **Journey Mapping**: Map your complete customer journey
2. **Emotional Analysis**: Identify emotional highs and lows
3. **Pain Point Prioritization**: Rank pain points by impact and frequency

## Key Takeaways
- Complete journey mapping reveals hidden opportunities
- Emotional mapping shows where users feel most frustrated or delighted
- Pain points often represent the biggest improvement opportunities
- Stakeholder alignment is crucial for journey improvement success
